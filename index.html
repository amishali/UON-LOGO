introduction

The University of Nairobi.turns 50 this year, having been inaugurated on July 1, 1970. The University plans a full year of celebrations and, for that, we need an logo. One that will capture the essence of the University as a great institution, bring out excitement and inspiration and blend well with or beside the Official University Logo.

The challenges

To design a logo that will be used by the University in all its communication and marketing material for the next year (you will be famous) as we celebrate 50 years. Look deep down into your inner creative self and bring out a design that will stand the test of time and pass the scrutiny of the judges who will pick the best design.

Eligibility

  1. The competition is open to University of Nairobi students only.
  2. Students must be in good standing.

What is the reward

A winner and two runners up shall be selected and awarded recognition and cash prizes.

  1. Winner – Kshs. 50,000
  2. 1st Runner Up – Kshs. 30,000
  3. 2nd Runner Up – Kshs. 10,000

The Fine Print

Awards shall be given with the understanding that the intellectual property captured in the submitted designs shall belong to the University.

How to apply

Apply through the online application form below. You will be required to upload your design as a single .pdf file. You must be logged into this Portal to be able to submit the application. Either Login or Create an Account from the menus above.
